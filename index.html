Full Job Description

Linkforce is a leading integrated maintenance, engineering, and project services delivery business. Founded in 2007, we've grown from a small crew of shutdown specialists to an operational workforce of over 2,800 personnel nationwide.

About Our Roles:

We're currently seeking talented individuals for various positions, including but not limited to:

  • Mechanical Fitters
  • Boilermakers
  • Electricians
  • Riggers
  • Scaffolders
  • Project Managers

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary packages with attractive hourly rates
  • Opportunities for career advancement and skill development
  • Work on exciting projects across Western Australia's Pilbara and Goldfields regions
  • A supportive and collaborative work environment that values mateship
  • Ongoing training and professional development opportunities
  • FIFO (Fly-In, Fly-Out) and residential opportunities available

Key Requirements:

  • Relevant trade qualifications or certifications for technical roles
  • Experience in the mining, industrial, or energy sectors (preferred but not always essential)
  • Strong commitment to safety and quality
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
  • Flexibility to work in various locations and on different projects

If you're passionate about making a difference and want to be part of a dynamic team that's shaping the future of Australia's resource sector, we want to hear from you!
